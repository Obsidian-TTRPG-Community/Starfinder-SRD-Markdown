# NANITE RIFLE
Nanite rifles can fire nanotech-infused darts at targets from extreme ranges. Although the darts deal little damage, the nanites eat into the target immediately and can cause grievous injuries. Nanites from tactical models target the site where they land, while an advanced rifleâ€™s nanites can spread farther within the target. An elite rifle allows its nanites to move even farther from the point struck, dealing more damage.
