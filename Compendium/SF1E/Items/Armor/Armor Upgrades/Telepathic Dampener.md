# Telepathic Dampener

**Source**:: _Near Space pg. 153_  
**Item Level** 5;
**Price**::  2,700  
**Slots** 1; **Armor Type** Any; **Bulk** L  
You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to saves against mind-affecting effects, but you can’t use or receive telepathic communication.
