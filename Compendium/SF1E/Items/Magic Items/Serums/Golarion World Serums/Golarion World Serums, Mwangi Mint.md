# Golarion World Serums, Mwangi Mint

**Source**:: _Ports of Call pg. 73_
**Level**:: 3
**Price**:: 250
**Bulk**:: L

### Description

Above text reading, “A garden of refreshment,” an image of a cartoonishly giant carnivorous plant with mint-like leaves graces the label of this light green beverage. The serum grants you a +2 insight bonus to saving throws against the spells, spell-like abilities, and supernatural abilities of plant creatures.
