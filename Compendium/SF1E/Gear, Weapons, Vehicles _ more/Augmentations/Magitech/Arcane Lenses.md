# ARCANE LENSES
**Source** _Starfinder Armory pg. 90_  
**Item Level** 11; **Price** 25,900; **System** Eyes  
Psychically active crystals replace the lenses of your eyes. You gain _arcane sight_, per the spell, as a continuous ability.
