# Kip up (COMBAT)

**Source**:: _Starfinder Core Rulebook pg. 158_  
You can stand up from prone in a hurry.

**Prerequisites**: Acrobatics 1 rank.

**Benefit**: You can stand from prone as a swift action, rather than a move action.
