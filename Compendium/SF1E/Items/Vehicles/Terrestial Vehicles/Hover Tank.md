# Hover Tank

**Price**::  227,000  
Gargantuan land and water vehicle (20 ft. wide, 20 ft. long, 8 ft. high)

**EAC** 28; **KAC** 30; **Cover** total cover  
**HP** 250 (125); **Hardness** 20  
**Attack (Collision)** 16d10 B (DC 16)
