# HOLO GRENADE

Commonly used as novelties, _holo grenades_ can create all sorts of illusions in a harmless but surprising and often amusing way. A holo version of the _wonder grenade_ (creating only an appearance of the listed effects) is popular among fey and children. However, _holo grenades_ have less innocent uses as well—criminals and infiltrators use _holo grenades_ as distractions.

A _holo grenade_ creates a preprogrammed illusion no larger than the blast radius. The illusion functions as if you had cast _holographic image_ with a level, caster level, and duration according to the grenade’s type.
