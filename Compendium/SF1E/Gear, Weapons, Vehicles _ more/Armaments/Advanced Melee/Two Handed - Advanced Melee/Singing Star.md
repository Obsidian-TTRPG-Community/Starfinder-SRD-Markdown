# SINGING STAR
An ancient and traditional pahtra weapon, the singing star consists of a long chain or rope with each end attached to a fist-sized metal slug covered in complex ridges and spikes. The wielder swings the chain, either tangling targets or using the metal sphere like a long flail, while the ridges cause the weapon to make an eerie and discomfiting whistle.
